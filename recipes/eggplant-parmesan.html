Eggplant Parmesan

Description

This easy Eggplant Parmesan recipe has layers of crispy, breaded eggplant slices that have been baked, instead of fried. The combination of crunchy bread crumbs, tomato sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese is guaranteed to please!

The classic combination of cheesy, herb-infused tomato sauce and crisp, oven-baked eggplant never gets old. This easy Eggplant Parmesan gets high marks in ease and flavor from our community of home cooks. Use straightforward pantry ingredients to create Italian-inspired magic with this top-rated recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 Large eggplants, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 Large eggs, beaten
  • 4 Cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 6 Cups spaghetti sauce, divided
  • 1 (16 ounce) package mozzarella cheese, shredded and divided
  • 1/2 Cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 Teaspoon dried basil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Dip eggplant slices in beaten egg, then in bread crumbs to coat. Place in single layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 5 minutes. Flip and bake for 5 more minutes.
  4. Spread spaghetti sauce to cover the bottom of the baking dish.
  5. Place a layer of eggplant slices in the sauce.
  6. Sprinkle with mozzarella and parmesan
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 with remaining ingredients, ending with a cheese layer. Sprinkle basil on top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, for about 35 minutes.

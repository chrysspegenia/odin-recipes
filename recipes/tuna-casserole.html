Tuna Noodle Casserole

Description

A Tuna Noodle Casserole recipe that provides a rich and creamy but not overwhelmingly heavy taste. While, the crunch from the toppings and fresh bite from the parsley create the perfect balance.


Nutritional Facts(per serving)

Calories: 481
Fat: 20g
Carbs: 51g
Protein: 28g
Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 (12ounce) Package egg noodles
  • 4-1/4 Teaspoons kosher salt, divided
  • 4 Tablespoons salted butter, divided
  • 5 Teaspoons olive oil, divided
  • 1 (8ounce) Package sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 Small white onion, minced
  • 1/4 Cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 Cups milk
  • 1-1/2 Cups chicken stock
  • 2 Tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 Teaspoon Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2 Teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1-1/2 Cups shredded white cheddar cheese
  • 1 Cup frozen sweet peas
  • 2 (6 ounce) Cans tuna, drained
  • 1 Cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1/4 Teaspoon ground paprika
  • 2 Tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-in baking dish.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add egg noodles and 3 teaspoons salt to the boiling water; cook until tender yet firm, about 6 min.
  3. Drain noodles. Return to pot and set aside.
  4. Heat 2 tablespoons butter and 1 tablespoon oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Add mushrooms and onion and cook until mushrooms are softened and onions are translucent.
  6. Add remaining butter; stir constantly until melted. Sprinkle flour over vegetable mix and cook, stirring constantly for 1 minute.
  7. Stir in milk and stock and bring to a simmer. And simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Stir in lemon juice, Dijon mustard, 1 teaspoon salt, and pepper.
  9. Add sauce mixture to reserved noodles. Stir in 1 cup cheddar cheese, peas, and tuna.
  10. Pour mixture into the prepared baking dish and sprinkle remaining cheddar cheese over the top.
  11. Stir together bread crumbs, 2 teaspoons oil, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and paprika in a small bowl. Sprinkle mixture evenly over the casserole.
  12. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and bubbly, 25-30 minutes. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
